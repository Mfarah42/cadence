Frequently asked

My on/off days look off by one.

Open Me → My rotation and check your anchor date — it should be the first day of one of your on-stretches. Adjust it and the whole calendar re-fills. Cadence computes days by the calendar, not the clock, so it stays correct across daylight saving and travel.

My rotation changed at work. Do I lose my history?

No. Set the new pattern from the date it changed; past days stay as they were.

I picked up or swapped a single day.

Tap that day in the Calendar and set it to Working, Off, or Vacation. Overrides win over the pattern.

Is anything locked behind a paywall?

No. Every useful feature is free, forever. The tip jar and Supporter Pack are optional, and the pack is cosmetic only.

How do I share my schedule with family?

Today → Share. Send a calendar file (.ics) they can import into Apple/Google/Outlook, or an image. They need no app and no account to view it.

Do reminders and alarms work?

Turn them on in Me → Schedule. The day-before reminder can also write into Apple Reminders. Shift alarms use the system alarm so they break through silent/Focus.

Where is my data?

On your device. See our Privacy Policy.
